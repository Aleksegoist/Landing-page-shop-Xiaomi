Xiaomi Himo C26

Electric Bike

Complete freedom of movement and reliable transport for travel at any distance

HIMO C26 is a new electric bike from Xiaomi, which is designed for both driving around the city and off-road. It has a powerful engine and a capacious battery, thanks to which it is able to drive on a single charge up to 100 km, which is definitely higher than most competitors. And the big tires with pneumatic tyres, disc brakes, 7-speed transmission and ergonomic seat make every ride much more enjoyable.
