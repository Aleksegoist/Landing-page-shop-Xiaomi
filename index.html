Xiaomi Himo C26

Electric Bike

Complete freedom of movement and reliable transport for travel at any distance

HIMO C26 is a new electric bike from Xiaomi, which is designed for both driving around the city and off-road. It has a powerful engine and a capacious battery, thanks to which it is able to drive on a single charge up to 100 km, which is definitely higher than most competitors. Comfort and safety from HIMO

The HIMO C26 electric bike will allow you to quickly and comfortably get to your place of work or study and not be tied to public transport. The bike has a solid construction and an impressive range to become a reliable vehicle in modern urban environments. A light and durable frame, 26-inch wheels and a 7-speed transmission system allow you to confidently move not only on an asphalt roadway, but also on a difficult country track.

Strong and lightweight frame

The HIMO C26 frame is made of durable and lightweight aluminum alloy and features high reliability, stability in emergency situations, excellent handling and comfortable movement on any road.

Combination of modes for extended range

The HIMO C26 electric bike has an extended range. Its 10,000 mAh battery with intelligent energy optimization system allows you to travel up to 60 km on a single charge in electric drive mode. When using physical force on some sections of the path, the cruising range increases to 100 km. The maximum travel speed of HIMO C26 is 25 km/h.

Reliable transmission

The 7-speed transmission system provides smooth and precise shifting, comfortable speed control and reliable operation. By shifting gears, you can drive up the hill with minimal effort and slide down safely.

All information on the LCD display

All information on the LCD display The large informative display placed on the steering column displays all the important information about the operation of the HIMO C26 in real time. You can find out the current speed of movement, the charge level or the distance traveled. The display is protected from external factors according to the IPX7 standard and is not afraid of rain and dust.

Comfortable saddle

The HIMO C26 electric bike is equipped with a bicycle saddle from the well-known brand Selle Royal. The three-dimensional gel structure of the saddle ensures optimal resilience and high riding comfort.
